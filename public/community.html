Tips for New Players: Voice Command Best Practices

Welcome to all the new AudioVR players! Here are some tips I've learned after completing 15+ mysteries that will help improve your voice command accuracy:

  1. 1. Speak clearly and at a consistent pace - The system adapts to your natural speech rhythm
  2. 2. Use the microphone 6-8 inches from your mouth - Too close causes audio distortion
  3. 3. Practice in a quiet environment first - Build confidence before trying noisy spaces
  4. 4. Learn command variations - "Examine knife" and "Look at blade" both work
  5. 5. Complete the voice training - It really does improve recognition accuracy

Tips & Accessibility Guides

Community-contributed tips to help you get the most out of AudioVR

Voice Commands

Speak Naturally

Use complete sentences like "I want to examine the knife" instead of just "examine knife"

Command Variations

Learn multiple ways to say the same thing: "talk to", "speak with", "ask", "question"

Audio Settings

Spatial Audio Setup

Use stereo headphones for best spatial positioning. Adjust balance in settings if needed.

Volume Balance

Set dialogue 20% louder than effects for clearer character conversations

Screen Reader

Navigation Mode

Use browse mode for scene descriptions, focus mode for interactions

Reading Speed

Slow down screen reader for detailed evidence descriptions
